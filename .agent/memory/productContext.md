# Product Context: Orendt Studios Mitarbeiter-Umfrage

## Why and What
The app aims to improve internal processes at Orendt Studios by collecting honest, anonymous feedback from employees. It replaces manual or less secure methods with a professional digital tool.

## User Experience
- **Employees**: Simple, distraction-free survey experience. Anonymous submission.
- **Admins**: Detailed analytics, easy question management, and secure access.
